About Us

Nanretji-bethel Hospital Services and Centre for Reproductive Health!

The founders and directors of Nanretji-bethel Hospital Services and Centre for Reproductive Health are Professor Josiah T Mutihir and Professor Patrick H Daru. They were joined by Professor Nuhu K Dakum. These individuals with their many years of experience; not only in medicine, but also in administration, form the core of Nanretji-bethel Hospital Services and Centre for Reproductive Health.

These veteran individuals and the team they have built ensure that the very best practices in Medicine are carried out. Being teachers and trainers themselves, everyone on board their team is taught the importance of Patient’s safety and comfort. This means that every staff is courteous and relates with the patient’s and other staff with utmost respect and strict professionalism while observing all the laid down protocols.

Vision

The vision of the hospital is to be a top rated Institution that outlives this generation, provides the best quality of care and positively impacts on society.

mission

Its mission is to promote a culture of excellence and discipline in its staff with regular training and to ensure prompt and efficient service delivery in order to positively impact families who are entrusted to our care.

OUR TEAM

The Best Health Specialist !

In order to achieve the above, Nanretji-bethel Hospital Services and Centre for Reproductive Health has a dedicated team of 37 permanent staff and 15 adhoc staff. Every staff must have requisite qualifications before they join us and thereafter receive regular on the job training. Remunerations are fair and paid in time so as to avoid complacency and distractions.

Allowances and off-times are also given to staff. This means that the team is highly motivated and efficient in its output. Every team member is aware of the vision and mission and all are constantly encouraged to work towards the target.
