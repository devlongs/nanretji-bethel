How we started

Nanretji-bethel Hospital Services and Centre for Reproductive Health, Mangu was founded in June 2015. It was initially located at Zaria Crescent but now is situate at No 7c, Hospital place, G.R.A, Jos. It began with a passion to serve without the restrictions of bureaucracy found in the public sector. Patients’ needs are paramount and they are promptly and efficiently attended to by some of the best professionals in their respective fields.
